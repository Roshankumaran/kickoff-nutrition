Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

At Kick Off Nutritions (accessible via kickoffnutrition.in), we operate as an online health and fitness supplement store offering premium gym supplements, protein powders, pre-workouts, creatine, vitamins, and wellness products. We value the trust you place in us and are dedicated to protecting your privacy and security. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless shopping experience, we collect information that helps us identify you and fulfill your orders efficiently. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Details: Name, shipping address, billing address, email address, and mobile phone number.
  • Order & Transaction Information: Products purchased, quantities, date of transaction, and total order value.
  • Technical & Usage Data: Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, device information, operating system, and browsing behavior on our store.

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the collected customer information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Order Processing & Fulfillment: Fulfilling purchases, verifying identities, generating shipping invoices, and updating you on delivery status.
  • Secure Payments: Facilitating transaction authentication via our checkout system.
  • Customer Support: Responding to product inquiries, issues, order changes, and delivery support requests.
  • Store Improvement: Analyzing website traffic patterns and product demand to optimize our inventory and user interface.

3. Sharing of Information

We believe in absolute transparency. Kick Off Nutritions does not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information with unauthorized third parties under any circumstances. We share your data only with third-party service providers essential for conducting our business:

  • Shipping Partners: To print shipping labels, route packages, and deliver supplements to your address within our target window of 3–4 working days.
  • Payment Gateway Providers: All payment transactions are processed through **Razorpay**, a secure and trusted industry-standard payment processor. We do not store or see your credit/debit card numbers, CVVs, or internet banking passwords.

4. Data Security Measures

We implement highly secure administrative, physical, and electronic security checks to guard against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal data. Our website uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption protocols for all data transfers. Secure online payments are processed safely through Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use small text files called cookies to enhance website navigation, recall items in your shopping cart, and evaluate site analytics. Cookies help us customize your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, doing so may affect the functionality of some components of the store (e.g., the cart system).

6. Customer Rights

As a valued user, you have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, request corrections to inaccurate details, or request deletion of your data from our customer records, subject to legal or transactional retention obligations (such as tax and audit trails).
